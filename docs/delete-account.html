⚠️ Important Notice

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

When you delete your account, all of your data will be permanently removed from our systems, including:

What Will Be Deleted:

  • Your account information (name, email, profile picture)
  • All uploaded screenshots
  • All extracted data and analysis results
  • Categories, tags, and organization data
  • Search history and preferences
  • All cloud backups and synced data

Processing Time:

  • We will process your deletion request within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete
  • During the processing period, your account will be deactivated

How to Request Deletion:

  • Click the button below to send a deletion request email
  • Include your registered email address in the request
  • Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • We may contact you to verify your identity before processing
